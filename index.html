What is Balshakti Seminar?

Balshakti Seminar is a special gathering where children learn Sahaj Yoga meditation, spiritual values, and the beautiful teachings of Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi. Through meditation, discussions, and cultural activities, children discover inner peace, innocence, and truth. Join us to awaken your inner spiritual light! 🌟

🙏 About Shri Mataji

🙏

Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi

Founder of Sahaj Yoga (1923 – 2011)

Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi was born on March 21, 1923, in Chindwara, India. Even as a child, she was extraordinary – wise, compassionate, and full of divine love.

She participated in India's freedom movement and stayed at Mahatma Gandhi's ashram. Gandhi was deeply impressed by her wisdom even at a young age.

On May 5, 1970, she opened the Sahasrara Chakra, making it possible for everyone to achieve self-realization through Sahaj Yoga meditation.

🧒 Childhood Stories

  • 🌸 She recognized spiritual truths even as a toddler
  • 🌸 She could identify pure and impure food by feeling vibrations
  • 🌸 She always helped other children and animals with love
  • 🌸 She was a brave participant in India's independence movement

🌸 Daily Values & Tips

✅ Good Habits

  • ●Meditate every morning for 10 minutes
  • ●Respect your parents and teachers always
  • ●Speak the truth with kindness
  • ●Help others without expecting anything
  • ●Keep your surroundings clean

❌ Qualities to Avoid

  • ●Anger and fighting with others
  • ●Telling lies or cheating
  • ●Being jealous of friends
  • ●Wasting time on screens too much
  • ●Disrespecting elders

🧘 Meditation Tips

  • ●Sit comfortably with your back straight
  • ●Place hands open on your lap, palms up
  • ●Close your eyes and focus on top of your head
  • ●Say inside: "I am the Spirit"
  • ●Feel the cool breeze on your hands

🌈 Positive Qualities

  • ●Innocence – Pure heart of Shri Ganesha
  • ●Compassion – Feel for all living beings
  • ●Generosity – Share what you have
  • ●Patience – Stay calm in all situations
